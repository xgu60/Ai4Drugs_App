Silico tool to predict antibiotic side effects of drug candidates

Please enter SMILES of your interested drugs!

1. Use DrugBank to get SMILES. 2. Or directly draw the struture using JSME editor.


You have to enable Java and JavaScritpt on your machine !






The predictions will be based on a pre-trained Random Forest model.
